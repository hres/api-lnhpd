Search licensed natural health products

From Health Canada


Note - The product information found within this database originates from organizations not subject to the Official Languages Act and is available in the language in which it was written and submitted to Health Canada.

Search criteria

Search for natural health products that have been issued a product licence by Health Canada.
You may search using 4 ways:
  1. Licence Holder
  2. Brand Name
  3. Medicinal Ingredient
  4. Natural Product Number (NPN) or Homeopathic Medicine Number (DIN-HM)

For more information on Regulatory Decision Summaries, click here: http://www.hc-sc.gc.ca/dhp-mps/prodpharma/rds-sdr/index-eng.php
